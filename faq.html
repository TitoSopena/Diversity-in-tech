Why is diversity in tech important?

Diversity in the technology industry is important because is the gateway thru which innovative ideas from diverse sources and cultures can help create solutions for the consumers for whom the tech products are made.

What should I do if I’m discriminated in the workplace?

There are a couple of advisable steps you could take in order to deal with discrimination in the workplace. First, you should review your company’s employee handbook, which should contain the company’s policy and guidelines on how to report discrimination. Secondly, you should contact your Human Resources (HR) department for assistance on how to file an official claim to report the discrimination. If the issue is not resolved adequately, you should contact your local Equal Employment Opportunity Commission (EEOC.) Lastly, it is vital that you document every instance in which you feel you are being discriminated. This could be any email, text, letters, or verbal incidents in which you experienced discrimination.

How can I get involved?

You should search for local tech meet ups in your area, or you could start your own local diverse tech meet up. The site Diversityintech.org will eventually provide a way for techs wanting to become more involved with other peers in the tech community.

What are some possible solutions?

There are many possible solutions too long to list. Generally speaking, solutions definitely depend on the issue for which you are seeking a solution. Studies have shown many benefits to having a diverse workforce. Mainly, financially. Also, when collaborating with peers in regular meet ups, open forums, and thru social media, it helps find practical and timely solutions to the issues being discussed.
