Diversity In Tech

Diversity In Tech aims to raise awareness on the gap of diversity in tech, mainly focusing on gender gap, race gap, and ethnicity gap. Diversity In Tech also provides information, resources, and seeks to assist anyone who has been a victim of discrimination in the workplace.
